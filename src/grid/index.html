Grid

By default, the Honeycomb grid is a 10 and 12 column fluid grid.

Spanning columns

To span columns, use the grid__col--span-x-of-y class, where x is the amount of columns you'd like to span, and y is the total amount of columns.

'Push'ing columns

To add space between columns you can 'push' columns in the grid. Use the grid__col--push-x-of-y class to do this.
